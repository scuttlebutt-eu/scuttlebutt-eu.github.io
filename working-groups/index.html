Grants Peach

Confirmed members:

Academic Peach

Purpose: Write papers about SSB, review each other's drafts, apply for academic funding.

Confirmed Members:

  • Erick

Diet SSB Peach

Purpose: Make SSB something that doesn't require a beefy machine. Target low-end smartphones, ARM notebooks (e.g. MNT Reform), Precursor, microcontrollers, etc.

Confirmed members:

  • SoapDog
  • Daan
  • Staltz

The Bun Depot

Purpose: Pushing clients forward, taking on incoming buns of various sizes wrt freelancing. The focus is funding-backed ecosystem work.

Confirmed members:

Social Hang Out Peach

Purpose: Low-key, low-frequency social hang-out
